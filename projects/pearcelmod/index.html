Pearcel Mod

Minecraft Mod

About

Pearcel Mod is a progression-based mod that mainly focuses around a central item, the Pearcel.

The Pearcel is a item that can be gathered by breaking leaves from trees with them dropping at a chance; the Pearcel Crook increases the chance.

From there, the player has various access to various items and blocks they can use while they progress throughout the game.
