Lightning Additions

Minecraft Mod

About

Lightning Additions is a mod developed by StormyMode and myself that adds various utilities to the game.

The majority of the mod is centrally revolves around the Tachyon. An unstable item in which it's powers can be harvested for unique tech.

More information can be found on CurseForge.
